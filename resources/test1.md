1. Employer creates campaign for job opening in Verity Portal
2. Employer manually uploads candidate resumes with emails
3. System sends invite: "Complete your profile for [Company - Job]"
4. Candidate downloads app
5. Candidate decides whether to engage:
    - If not interested: candidate uninstalls the app (exits flow)
    - If interested: continues to next step
6. Candidate provides additional info/evidence through the app
7. System checks if profile is complete:
    - If incomplete: candidate repeats step 6 until all required info is provided
    - If complete: continues to verification
8. System runs verifications in parallel:
    - Verify identity
    - Check credentials
9. Employer sees progressive results in portal as verifications complete
10. On hire: employer triggers full background check
